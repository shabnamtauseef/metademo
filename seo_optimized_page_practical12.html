What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the practice of improving the ranking of a website in search engine results.

How Meta Tags Help with SEO

Meta tags provide metadata about a webpage and are used by search engines to understand the content of the page. They include the title, description, and keywords.
